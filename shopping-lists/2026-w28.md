# Frukt & Grönt
- [ ] ca 1 kg [Potatis](/ingredients/potatis.md)
- [ ] 3 st [Gul lök](/ingredients/gul-lok.md) (varav ~200 g smålök till coq au vin)
- [ ] 5 klyftor [Vitlök](/ingredients/vitlok.md) (ca 1 knippe)
- [ ] 1 st [Aubergine](/ingredients/aubergine.md)
- [ ] 1 st [Zucchini](/ingredients/zucchini.md)
- [ ] 2 st [Paprika](/ingredients/paprika.md)
- [ ] 1 knippe [Sparris](/ingredients/sparris.md)
- [ ] 2 st [Tomat](/ingredients/tomat.md)
- [ ] 1 st [Färsk chili](/ingredients/farsk-chili.md)
- [ ] 1 st [Citron](/ingredients/citron.md)
- [ ] 1 kruka [Persilja](/ingredients/persilja.md)
- [ ] 1 kruka [Timjan](/ingredients/timjan.md) (eller torkad)
- [ ] 2 st [Sallad](/ingredients/sallad.md)
- [ ] 250 g [Svamp](/ingredients/svamp.md)

# Kött & Chark
- [x] ca 1,2 kg [Picanha](/ingredients/picanha.md) (finns hemma)
- [ ] 600 g [Nötfärs](/ingredients/notfars.md)
- [ ] 800 g [Kycklingfilé](/ingredients/kycklingfile.md) (gärna lårfilé)
- [ ] 140 g [Bacon](/ingredients/bacon.md)

# Mejeri
- [ ] 4 skivor [Riven ost](/ingredients/riven-ost.md) (cheddar)
- [ ] 1 pkt [Smör](/ingredients/smor.md) (till stekning och coq au vin)

# Bröd
- [ ] 4 st [Hamburgerbröd](/ingredients/hamburgerbrod.md)

# Skafferi
- [ ] 5 dl [Rödvin](/ingredients/rodvin.md) (torrt, till coq au vin)
- [ ] ca 1,5 dl [Olivolja](/ingredients/olivolja.md) (chimichurri + grillgrönsaker)
- [ ] 2 msk [Rödvinsvinäger](/ingredients/rodvinsvinager.md)
- [ ] 3 dl [Buljong](/ingredients/buljong.md) (höns eller kött)
- [ ] 2 msk [Tomatpuré](/ingredients/tomatpure.md)
- [ ] 2 msk [Vetemjöl](/ingredients/vetemjol.md)
- [ ] 2 st [Lagerblad](/ingredients/lagerblad.md)
- [ ] [Oregano](/ingredients/oregano.md) (torkad)
- [ ] [Ketchup](/ingredients/ketchup.md)
- [ ] [Senap](/ingredients/senap.md)
- [ ] [Saltgurka](/ingredients/saltgurka.md)
- [ ] [Salt](/ingredients/salt.md) (grovt havssalt till torrsaltningen)
- [ ] [Svartpeppar](/ingredients/svartpeppar.md)

> Skafferistaplar som salt, peppar, olja, senap och ketchup har du kanske redan — bocka av det du har hemma.
